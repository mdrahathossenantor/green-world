Who We Are?

We have only one home, It’s called planet Earth.

Trees are the main oxygen source of our Earth, by planting them we can reduce the CO2 level. According to science and research, forest reforestation is the number one answer for stopping global warming. That is why we need to take action now. We are committed to plant ten millions trees in the next 3 years in several countries all over the world.

We buy private lands, take samples from the agriculture land and make it into a beautiful forest. We make sure that those fields and trees are protected and nobody will ever touch it. As a Hungarian startup, (at the beginning) we plant the trees in lands of Hungary but we are fully committed to spread the word and grow our project into a global mission by planting in many countries all over the world.

We Work For Environment

Did you know that planting a tree can haveahuge impact on our planet? Trees produce oxygen and provide habitat for animals who call the forest home.By planting trees you can help combat deforestation and encourage biodiversity! Plant one tree today and help supportahealthy future for our planet!

More About What We Do

We Work For People.

Our team is a bunch of motivated entrepreneurs who want to secure the future of the planet. Trees are the main oxygen source of our Earth, by planting them we can reduce the CO2 level. As a devoted team who really wants to do something to our environment, we are constantly working with foresters and planting, growing and protecting our Plant A Tree forests for a better and greener tomorrow! Join our mission and let’s give Nature a little back of how much it’s given to us.

Our goal is to plant 5 MILLION Trees worldwide in the next 3 years. To stop and turn around deforestation is the one of the most effective ways of a healthy ecosystem on Earth and to reduce CO2. Creating new forests is what we are assigned to DO, so our wonderful world can breath again. We buy private lands, take samples from the agriculture land and make it into a beautiful forest. Make sure that those fields and trees are protected and nobody will ever touch them.

Another great way to protect the future of our planet is to go green at home! Start by switching all of your household products to natural,non-toxic alternatives. You can also install solar panels and energy-efficient appliances to reduce your energy bills.Small changes like this can makeabig impact-and you'll feel great knowing that you're doing your part to help protect the environment!
